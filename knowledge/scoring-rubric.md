# Candidate Scoring Rubric

## Match Score Calculation (0-100)

### Technical Skills — 35 points max
- All required skills present with 3+ years: 30-35
- Most required skills, some gaps: 20-29
- Partial match, significant gaps: 10-19
- Minimal match: 0-9

### Experience Level — 25 points max
- Exceeds YOE requirement + relevant industry: 22-25
- Meets YOE requirement: 15-21
- Slightly under but strong trajectory: 8-14
- Significantly under: 0-7

### Education — 15 points max
- Exceeds requirements + relevant certs: 13-15
- Meets requirements: 9-12
- Partial match or equivalent experience: 5-8
- Does not meet: 0-4

### Culture Signals — 15 points max
- Strong company-size and industry fit: 13-15
- Reasonable fit: 9-12
- Unclear or partial fit: 5-8
- Likely mismatch: 0-4

### Bonus Factors — 10 points max
- Local candidate + immediately available + referral: 8-10
- Two of three: 5-7
- One factor: 2-4
- None: 0-1

## Thresholds
- 80+: Strong match — fast-track to interview
- 60-79: Good match — advance to pre-screening
- 40-59: Marginal — flag for human review
- Below 40: Weak match — archive with reason logged
