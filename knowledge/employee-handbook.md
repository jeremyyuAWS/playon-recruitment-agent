# PlayOn Sports — Employee Handbook Summary

## Employment Basics
- **Employment Type**: At-will (either party can end employment at any time)
- **Work Hours**: Core hours 10am-4pm ET, flexible start/end
- **Overtime**: Non-exempt employees eligible per FLSA
- **Pay Schedule**: Bi-weekly, direct deposit required

## Code of Conduct
- Treat all colleagues, candidates, and partners with respect
- Zero tolerance for harassment, discrimination, or retaliation
- Report concerns to HR or anonymous ethics hotline
- Conflicts of interest must be disclosed to manager and HR

## Leave Policies

### PTO
- Accrual-based, available after 90-day probation
- Maximum carryover: 5 days to next calendar year
- Payout of unused PTO upon departure (where required by state law)
- Request via HRIS, manager approval required for >3 consecutive days

### Sick Leave
- Available immediately upon hire
- No doctor's note required for 1-3 days; required for 4+
- Can be used for personal illness, family care, or mental health days

### Parental Leave
- Eligible after 12 months of employment
- 12 weeks paid (birthing parent), 6 weeks paid (non-birthing parent)
- Runs concurrently with FMLA where applicable
- Return-to-work accommodations (flexible schedule for 4 weeks)

### FMLA
- Eligible: 12+ months employed, 1,250+ hours worked
- 12 weeks unpaid, job-protected leave per year
- Qualifying reasons: serious health condition, family care, military family

## Performance Management
- **Probation**: First 90 days — weekly check-ins with manager
- **Reviews**: Quarterly lightweight check-ins, annual formal review
- **PIPs**: Performance improvement plans are 60 days with clear metrics
- **Promotions**: Annual promotion cycle (March), off-cycle for exceptional cases

## Workplace Policies
- **Remote Work**: Hybrid schedule, must be within commuting distance of office
- **Travel**: Pre-approved by manager, expenses reimbursed within 30 days
- **Equipment**: Company property, must be returned upon departure
- **Data Security**: No company data on personal devices, mandatory security training

## Separation
- **Voluntary Resignation**: 2 weeks notice requested (4 weeks for directors+)
- **Involuntary Termination**: Immediate for cause, 2 weeks severance otherwise
- **Exit Interview**: Conducted by HR within last week
- **COBRA**: 18 months of health coverage continuation available
