# Interview Question Bank

## Behavioral Questions (All Roles)

### Collaboration
- "Describe a project where you had to work with a team that had conflicting priorities. How did you align everyone?"
- "Tell me about a time you received critical feedback. What did you do with it?"
- Follow-ups: What was the outcome? What would you do differently?

### Problem Solving
- "Walk me through the most complex problem you've solved in the last year. How did you approach it?"
- "Tell me about a time you had to make a decision with incomplete information."
- Follow-ups: What alternatives did you consider? How did you validate your approach?

### Ownership
- "Describe a time you identified a problem no one asked you to solve. What did you do?"
- "Tell me about a project that failed. What was your role and what did you learn?"

### Adaptability
- "Tell me about a time your priorities changed suddenly. How did you handle it?"
- "Describe a situation where you had to learn something new quickly to deliver results."

## Technical Questions — Engineering

### Junior (0-2 years)
- "Explain how HTTP works at a high level. What happens when you type a URL in a browser?"
- "Write a function that [role-specific problem]. Walk me through your thinking."
- "How do you approach debugging a bug you've never seen before?"

### Mid (3-5 years)
- "How do you decide between building a new service vs. extending an existing one?"
- "Describe your approach to writing tests. What do you test and why?"
- "Tell me about a performance issue you diagnosed and fixed."

### Senior (5-8 years)
- "How would you design a system to handle [PlayOn-relevant scenario: live sports data streaming]?"
- "What's your approach to technical debt? How do you balance it with feature work?"
- "Describe a time you mentored a junior engineer through a difficult technical challenge."

### Staff (8+ years)
- "How do you evaluate build vs. buy decisions for critical infrastructure?"
- "Tell me about a time you influenced technical strategy across multiple teams."
- "How do you handle disagreements with other senior technical leaders?"

## Culture Add Questions (All Roles)
- "What perspective or experience do you bring that's different from what we might already have?"
- "What's something you're passionate about outside of work that shapes how you work?"
- "What does a great team culture look like to you, and how do you contribute to it?"

## Questions to Avoid (Illegal/Inappropriate)
- Age, birthdate, graduation year
- Marital/family status, pregnancy plans
- Religion, national origin, citizenship (only: "Are you authorized to work in the US?")
- Disability or health conditions
- Arrest record (convictions only, where legally permitted)
- Salary history (banned in many states)
