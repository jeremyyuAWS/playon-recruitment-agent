# Interview Panel Matrix

## Engineering Roles

| Interview | Duration | Interviewer Role | Competencies Evaluated |
|-----------|----------|-----------------|----------------------|
| Phone Screen | 30 min | Recruiter | Communication, motivation, comp alignment |
| Technical Assessment | 90 min | — (async) | Coding ability, problem solving |
| Technical Interview | 60 min | Senior+ Engineer (same team) | System design, code quality, technical depth |
| Behavioral Interview | 45 min | Engineering Manager | Collaboration, ownership, conflict resolution |
| Hiring Manager | 45 min | Hiring Manager | Role fit, team dynamics, growth potential |
| Executive (Staff+ only) | 30 min | VP Engineering | Strategic thinking, leadership, culture add |

### Panel Assignment Rules
- Technical interviewer must be same or higher level than candidate
- At least 1 interviewer should NOT be on the candidate's future team (reduces bias)
- No more than 5 interviews total (candidate experience)
- Maximum 2 interviews per day

## Interviewer Availability

### Primary Interview Days
- **Tuesday-Thursday**: Preferred interview scheduling window
- **Monday**: Okay for phone screens only
- **Friday**: Avoid (lower candidate show rate, lower energy)

### Interviewer Load Limits
- Maximum 4 interviews per interviewer per week
- No back-to-back interviews (15-min buffer minimum)
- Interviewers should block recurring "interview slots" on their calendars

## Feedback SLAs
- Feedback submitted within 24 hours of interview
- No interviewer communication about candidates before debrief
- Debrief scheduled within 48 hours of last interview completing
