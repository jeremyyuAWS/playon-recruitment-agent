# PlayOn Sports — Hiring SLA Targets

## Pipeline Stage SLAs

| Stage | Target Duration | Escalation Trigger |
|-------|----------------|-------------------|
| Requisition approval | 5 business days | Day 6 → VP notification |
| JD optimization + posting | 2 business days | Day 3 → recruiter manager |
| Resume screening (AI) | 24 hours from posting | 48 hours → system alert |
| Pre-screening outreach | 48 hours from screen pass | 72 hours → recruiter reminder |
| Pre-screen completion | 5 business days | Day 7 → auto follow-up |
| Interview scheduling | 3 business days from pre-screen pass | Day 5 → hiring manager ping |
| Interview completion | 10 business days from first interview | Day 12 → recruiter + HM check-in |
| Feedback collection | 24 hours post-interview | 48 hours → auto-reminder to interviewer |
| Hiring decision | 3 business days from final interview | Day 5 → recruiter escalation |
| Offer generation + approval | 3 business days from decision | Day 5 → HR escalation |
| Offer response | 5 business days | Day 4 → recruiter follow-up |
| Background check | 5-7 business days | Day 10 → vendor escalation |
| Start date | Within 4 weeks of offer acceptance | If delayed → weekly check-in |

## Overall Targets

| Metric | Target | Stretch Goal |
|--------|--------|-------------|
| Time-to-fill (all roles) | 45 days | 35 days |
| Time-to-fill (technical) | 55 days | 42 days |
| Time-to-fill (non-technical) | 35 days | 28 days |
| Offer acceptance rate | 85% | 90% |
| Quality of hire (90-day retention) | 95% | 98% |
| Candidate NPS | 50+ | 70+ |
| Internal fill rate | 20% | 30% |
| Cost per hire | <$5,000 | <$3,500 |

## Recruiter Capacity
- 3 recruiters managing ~57 hires each per year
- Sustainable load: ~15 active requisitions per recruiter
- Above 20 active reqs: flag for contractor support or prioritization

## Reporting Cadence
- **Daily**: Pipeline movement alerts (Slack)
- **Weekly**: Recruiter standup — stalled candidates, blockers
- **Monthly**: Hiring velocity report to leadership
- **Quarterly**: Full metrics review with C-suite
