All templates are pre-approved by HR and legal. Customize the bracketed sections only.

## Guidelines for All Rejections

### Do
- Use the candidate's first name
- Reference something specific to their application or conversation
- Be honest about the reason (in general terms)
- Leave the door open for future opportunities (only if genuine)
- Send during business hours, Monday-Thursday

### Don't
- Send on Fridays or before holidays
- Use "unfortunately" as the first word
- Compare the candidate to the person who was selected
- Promise future consideration you don't intend to follow through on
- Include compensation details or the selected candidate's qualifications
- Use passive voice to avoid ownership ("it was decided" → "we decided")
