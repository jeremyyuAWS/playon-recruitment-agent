# PlayOn Sports — Recruitment Context

## Company Overview
PlayOn Sports is a sports technology company. The recruiting team is 3 people managing 170+ hires per year.

## The Core Challenge
- Single technical role postings can receive 2,500+ applications within 48 hours
- Manual review of every resume within a week is physically impossible
- The team needs automated stack-ranking with human oversight

## Current ATS
- **Lever** is the system of record
- All candidate data lives in Lever
- Any AI tooling must integrate bidirectionally (pull applications, push rankings)
- Recruiters should never leave Lever to use AI features
