# PlayOn Sports — Benefits Guide

## Health & Wellness
- **Medical**: PPO and HDHP options, company covers 80% of premiums
- **Dental**: Preventive care 100% covered, major procedures 80%
- **Vision**: Annual exam + $200 frame/lens allowance
- **HSA**: Company contributes $500/year for HDHP participants
- **Mental Health**: EAP program, therapy sessions covered (6/year)
- **Wellness Stipend**: $50/month for gym, fitness apps, wellness activities

## Time Off
- **PTO**: 15 days/year (years 1-3), 20 days (years 4-7), 25 days (8+)
- **Sick Leave**: 10 days/year (separate from PTO)
- **Holidays**: 10 company holidays + 2 floating holidays
- **Parental Leave**: 12 weeks paid (birthing parent), 6 weeks (non-birthing parent)
- **Bereavement**: 5 days (immediate family), 3 days (extended family)
- **Jury Duty**: Full pay for duration of service

## Financial
- **401(k)**: Company matches 4% of salary (immediate vesting)
- **Life Insurance**: 2x salary (company-paid), supplemental available
- **Short-Term Disability**: 60% of salary, up to 12 weeks
- **Long-Term Disability**: 60% of salary, company-paid
- **Commuter Benefits**: Pre-tax transit and parking

## Professional Development
- **Learning Budget**: $2,000/year for courses, conferences, certifications
- **Tuition Reimbursement**: Up to $5,250/year (tax-free limit)
- **Internal Training**: Role-specific onboarding tracks via LMS

## Perks
- **Remote Work**: Hybrid policy — 3 days office, 2 days remote (role-dependent)
- **Equipment**: Laptop + monitor provided, $500 home office stipend
- **Team Events**: Quarterly team outings, annual company offsite
- **Sports Perks**: Free access to PlayOn streaming events
