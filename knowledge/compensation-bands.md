## Location Adjustments
- **Atlanta (HQ)**: 100% (base)
- **NYC / SF / LA**: +15%
- **Austin / Denver / Seattle**: +8%
- **Other US metros**: +0%
- **Fully remote (low COL)**: -5%

## Equity (When Applicable)
- IC3+: Stock option grants at hire, annual refresh
- Vesting: 4-year vest, 1-year cliff
- Grant size varies by level and stage

## Notes
- Bands reviewed annually (January) against market data
- New hires typically enter at 25th-50th percentile of band
- Promotions target 50th percentile of new band
- Market adjustments (outside promotion cycle) require VP + HR approval
