# Employer Branding

## Instructions

Create compelling, authentic messaging about why candidates should join PlayOn Sports — tailored to specific roles, audiences, and channels.

### Data Sources
- Glassdoor/Indeed reviews (themes, not individual reviews)
- Recent company wins (product launches, partnerships, funding, awards)
- Employee testimonials (volunteered, not coerced)
- Benefits and perks differentiators
- Team-specific culture signals from hiring manager intake
- Career growth data (internal promotion rate, average tenure)
- Tech stack and engineering culture (for technical roles)

### Brand Pillars

Build messaging around PlayOn's core EVP (Employee Value Proposition):

1. **Mission**: Connecting fans to the sports they love — every hire impacts millions of viewers
2. **Growth**: [X]% revenue growth, expanding product lines, career paths
3. **Technology**: [Describe tech stack, scale, interesting problems]
4. **Culture**: [Team size sweet spot, collaboration style, work-life balance data]
5. **Impact**: Your work is visible — [specific examples of employee impact]

### Role-Specific Messaging

#### For Engineers
- Scale challenges (streaming to X concurrent viewers)
- Tech stack and autonomy
- Engineering culture (code review, testing, deploy frequency)
- Open source contributions
- Conference/learning budget

#### For Product/Design
- User impact (X million fans, Y schools)
- Data-informed decision making
- Cross-functional collaboration model
- Product vision and roadmap influence

#### For Sales/Business
- Market position and growth trajectory
- Compensation structure and earning potential
- Client portfolio and deal sizes
- Career advancement data

#### For Operations
- Process improvement opportunities
- Tool modernization initiatives
- Growth means new challenges constantly
- Cross-team visibility

```

### Elevator Pitch (30 seconds)
[2-3 sentences tailored to what this audience cares about]

### LinkedIn InMail / Sourcing Message
[Personalized outreach template with role-specific hooks]

[4-5 bullet points for the JD]

When the candidate asks "Why should I join?":
1. [Point with specific proof]
2. [Point with specific proof]
3. [Point with specific proof]

### Objection Handling
| Common Objection | Response |
|-----------------|----------|
| "Never heard of PlayOn" | [Response with metrics] |
| "I want a bigger company" | [Response about growth stage] |
| "Compensation seems low" | [Response about total comp + growth] |
| "Is it stable?" | [Response with financial health data] |
```

### Channel-Specific Adaptation
- **LinkedIn**: Professional, metric-driven, career-growth focused
- **Twitter/X**: Casual, culture-forward, mission-driven
- **Job boards**: Comprehensive, SEO-optimized, benefits-heavy
- **Referral asks**: Personal, team-specific, "you'd love working with us because..."
- **Campus/early career**: Mission, learning, mentorship, fun

### Rules
- All messaging must be truthful and verifiable — never exaggerate
- Don't copy competitors' messaging — be authentically PlayOn
- Refresh messaging quarterly (stale brands feel stale)
- A/B test outreach messages — track response rates by variation
- Never use stock phrases: "fast-paced environment", "wear many hats", "like a family"
- Always include at least one specific, concrete detail (number, product, team name)
